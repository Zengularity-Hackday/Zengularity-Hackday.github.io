hackday

once a month at Zengularity since 2011

The Zengularity Hackdays take place the last friday of each month. With this day we celebrate the web and all its possibilities, and we try to make it even more awesome. We love the web and this website is our love letter.

You'll find here the projects we contributed back to the community. Please bear in mind those projects are prototypes and the products of a single day.
